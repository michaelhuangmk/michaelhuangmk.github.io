Duolingo Games Concept
Winter 2017

I completed a concept case study on better engaging users through gamified interactions and motivation, as well as encouraging greater participation within the Clubs feature.

This project was conducted as part of a 2-week sprint, where I iterated through the design process from discovery to delivery, with the final product as a prototype of the feature concept.

I am in no way affiliated with Duolingo.

Cornell Information Science Department
Summer 2017 - Fall 2017

I worked in a team of 3 designers to develop a web application for smallholder farmers in Latin America to calculate their annual costs of coffee production.

This project involved an end-to-end design process, starting from initial user research to iterative developement to product launch and on-site usability testing in Peru.

In colaboration with Fair Trade USA.

Life Changing Labs
Summer 2017

As the design lead for the Lokals team, I worked part-time to produce high-fidelity mockups for a web-based travel startup connecting travelers to unique excursions guided by local residents.

Deliverables for this project included high-fidelity mockups of the landing page and traveler/guide interaction flows, in addition to a basic prototype demo for NUX onboarding.
