Duolingo Games Concept
Winter 2017

I completed a case study on better engaging users through gamified interaction and motivation in the education app Duolingo, in addition to increasing participation within the Clubs feature.

This project was part of a 2-week sprint where I iterated through the design process, culminating in a prototype of the feature concept.

This was a side project done purely out of interest. I am in no way affiliated with Duolingo.

Cornell Information Science Department
Summer 2017 — Fall 2017

I worked in a team of 3 designers to develop a personal finance tool for smallholder farmers in Latin America to calculate their yearly costs of coffee production.

This project involved an end-to-end design process, from initial user research and iterative developement to product launch and on-site usability testing in Peru.

In colaboration with Fair Trade USA.

Life Changing Labs
Summer 2017

As the design lead for the Lokals team, I worked part-time to produce visual assets for a travel startup connecting travelers to unique excursions guided by local residents.
